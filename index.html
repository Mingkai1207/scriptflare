FAQ

      

        

          

            What is a faceless YouTube channel?
            ▼
          

          
A faceless YouTube channel publishes videos where the creator never appears on camera. Content is delivered through voiceovers paired with stock footage, animations, slideshows, or screen recordings. This format is popular for finance, history, motivation, true crime, and educational channels.

        

        

          

            How long are the generated scripts?
            ▼
          

          
You control the length — 5 minutes (~750 words), 8 minutes (~1,200 words), 10 minutes (~1,500 words), 12 minutes (~1,800 words), or 15 minutes (~2,250 words). Each length is calibrated for optimal YouTube ad break placement and watch time.

        

        

          

            Is this better than just using ChatGPT?
            ▼
          

          
Yes, significantly. ChatGPT gives you generic text. ScriptFlare uses a specialized YouTube prompting system that knows about hooks, open loops, retention curves, B-roll pacing, and CTA placement. You'd need to write a multi-paragraph prompt to get close to what ScriptFlare does automatically — and you'd still need to iterate. ScriptFlare gives you a production-ready script in one click, calibrated for your niche, your audience, and your target length.

More being added regularly.

Every script is generated fresh — no two scripts are identical. You own the scripts you generate and can use them commercially on your YouTube channel, voiceover platforms, or anywhere else.

        

        

          

            Will YouTube detect my videos as AI-generated?
            ▼
          

          
ScriptFlare writes scripts designed to sound like a knowledgeable human — not robotic AI text. YouTube's content detection focuses on video quality and audience engagement, not scriptwriting tools. Thousands of monetized faceless channels use AI-assisted scripting. Your niche expertise, voiceover delivery, and editing style are what make the content uniquely yours.

        

        

          

            Can I monetize a channel built with ScriptFlare scripts?
            ▼
          

          
Yes, absolutely. YouTube's monetization policies are about channel behavior, audience engagement, and content quality — not which tools you use to write scripts. ScriptFlare-generated scripts are 100% original, produced fresh on demand, and fully compliant with YouTube's terms of service. The script is just a starting point — you control the final video.

        

        

          

            Does it work with AI voiceover tools like ElevenLabs or Murf?
            ▼
          

          
Yes — ScriptFlare scripts are written to sound natural when spoken aloud, which makes them ideal for AI voiceover tools like ElevenLabs, Murf, or Play.ht. Just copy your script, paste it into your voiceover tool, and record. The B-roll cues are clearly marked so you always know exactly what footage to overlay.

        

        

          

            How many videos can I publish per month with ScriptFlare?
            ▼
          

          
With Pro, there's no limit — generate as many scripts as you need. Most successful faceless channels publish 3–5 videos per week, which typically means 12–20 scripts per month. At $19/month, that works out to under $2 per script. Compare that to $100–500 per script from a freelance writer, and the math is obvious. The only limit is how fast you can film, edit, and upload.

        

        

          

            What video length is best for growing a new faceless channel?
            ▼
          

          
For new channels, 8–12 minute videos tend to perform best. They're long enough to qualify for mid-roll ads once you hit 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours, but short enough to maintain high audience retention while your channel is still building trust with the algorithm. Finance and true crime channels can push 12–15 minutes once they have a proven audience. ScriptFlare auto-suggests the optimal length when you pick your niche.

        

        

          

            Does ScriptFlare support YouTube Shorts?
            ▼
          

          
Yes — ScriptFlare has a dedicated YouTube Shorts mode that generates a 60-second (~150 word) script optimized for vertical video. The Shorts prompt skips sections and headers entirely, focusing on a punchy 2–3 word hook, single-message delivery, 2–3 visual cues, and a crisp verbal CTA. Select "YouTube Short (60s)" from the Video Length dropdown to use it.

        

        

          

            Can I repurpose my YouTube script for social media?
            ▼
          

          
Yes — Pro users get access to the Social Media Repurposer. After generating a script, click the "📱 Repurpose" button to automatically generate 3 tweet-sized posts, a LinkedIn post, and an Instagram caption with hashtags — all based on your script's topic and key points. Each post can be copied with one click.

        

        

          

            Can ScriptFlare automatically improve a low-quality script?
            ▼
          

          
Yes — Pro users see a "✨ Improve" button in the output panel. It analyzes your script's quality report, identifies specific gaps (missing hook, low B-roll count, no open loop, no CTA), and sends targeted improvement instructions to the AI. The improved version is rendered in-place — no need to regenerate from scratch.

        

        

          

            Can I speak my video topic instead of typing it?
            ▼
          

          
Yes — click the 🎙️ microphone button next to the topic field to use voice input. ScriptFlare uses your browser's built-in Speech Recognition API (available in Chrome and Edge) to capture your idea. Just speak naturally — your words are transcribed into the topic field automatically. Then hit generate as normal.
